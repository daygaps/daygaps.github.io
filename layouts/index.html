Private by architecture

Your data lives in your iCloud. Nowhere else.

Your areas, projects, and days sync between your devices through your own private iCloud zone. There are no {{ partial "brand-inline.html" . }} servers, no account to create, and no analytics watching you work. The privacy page is short because there is nothing to disclose.

And when you want your plans as text, the bridge writes them to a folder as plain YAML: readable in any editor, diffable in git, and ready to hand to the AI assistant of your choice. The app is meant to be operated with an AI, on your terms.
