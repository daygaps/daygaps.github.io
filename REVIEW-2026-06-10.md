## Editorial decisions you may want to overrule

- Concepts hierarchy diagram is deliberately abstract (typographic tree +
  checkboxes on palette tokens), not faked app chrome: it explains structure
  without promising pixels, which keeps mirror-the-app intact while the UI
  is still moving. If you'd rather have a real screenshot there later, the
  figure swaps out cleanly.
- Pricing copy appears in three places (landing CTA, faq "What it costs",
  beta "When v1.0 ships") including the back-to-school discount. Trim if
  you'd rather not pre-announce the discount.
- Terms page leans on Apple's standard EULA and plain-language summaries.
  Worth a careful read; a lawyer pass is your call.
- Privacy page states zero analytics/telemetry. True for 1.0; if opt-in
  telemetry arrives in 1.x, the page needs a dated update then.
